Our story

Clothes that carry a place with them.

Four students, one school project, and a shared passion for heritage—that is how Indus Thread was born. What started as a classroom assignment quickly evolved into a blooming fashion brand. Driven by a desire to make traditional aesthetics relevant for modern wardrobes, the founders looked to India's vast cultural landscape for inspiration.They realized that while Gen Z loved contemporary style, they lacked a meaningful connection to their roots. Indus Thread bridged this gap. The brand infuses rich Indian history, complex textile patterns, and vibrant regional art into wearable, everyday streetwear and modern silhouettes.By sourcing ethically and honoring timeless craftsmanship, Indus Thread transforms ancient stories into modern style statements. Today, the brand stands as a tribute to youth-driven entrepreneurship, proving that true style is always rooted in culture.

Why we exist

Roots, not references

Most "ethnic streetwear" treats Indian PRINTS as a backdrop. We start from the PRINTS itself — its geometry, its history, the craft behind it — and build a garment around that, not the other way round.

How we make it

Small batches, real workshops

Every print run is produced in limited batches with print and stitching units across Delhi-NCR. Smaller runs mean less waste, tighter quality control, and fairer pay per piece.

What we stand for

The fine print, made plain

Fair pay

Print and stitching partners are paid above standard piece rates, with on-time payments — every batch.

Honest fabric

100% cotton tees 250-GSM and heavyweight cotton-fleece hoodies. No blends marketed as "premium."

Credit where due

Every product page names the print technique and region it draws from — never just "ethnic print."

Made to last

Tested for wash and wear so the print outlasts the trend cycle, not just the first wash.
